      Evidence-Based Routine (Push/Pull/Legs)

      Research shows training each muscle group 2x/week maximizes hypertrophy compared to 1x/week. Focus on progressive overload.

      Push

      • Bench Press: 3x5-8
      • OHP: 3x8-10
      • Incline DB Press: 3x8-12
      • Lateral Raises: 3x12-15
      • Tricep Pushdowns: 3x10-15

      Pull

      • Barbell Rows: 3x5-8
      • Pull-ups: 3x8-12
      • Face Pulls: 3x12-15
      • Barbell Curls: 3x10-12
      • Hammer Curls: 3x10-12

      Legs

      • Squats: 3x5-8
      • RDLs: 3x8-10
      • Leg Press: 3x10-12
      • Leg Curls: 3x10-15
      • Calf Raises: 4x15-20
      Source: Schoenfeld, B. J., et al. (2016). Effects of Resistance Training Frequency on Measures of Muscle Hypertrophy. Sports Medicine.

      Addiction & Habit Recovery

      Replacing negative dopamine loops with disciplined action. If you fail, acknowledge it, reset, and start again immediately.

      Psychology of Quitting:

      • Identify Triggers: Boredom, stress, and isolation.
      • Implementation Intention: "If I feel tempted to watch porn, then I will immediately do 20 pushups."
      • Forgive & Recommit: A relapse is a lapse in execution, not character. Start over without shame.
